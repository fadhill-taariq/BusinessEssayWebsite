Charles Clinton Spaulding - The Father of Black Business

Welcome to my blog! - Taariq Fadhill

Introduction

Charles Clinton Spaulding was a rook in the chess board that was navigating white America; making necessary bounds to solidify himself and his community into the free world Emancipation offered them. Spaulding’s success in the North Carolina Mutual Insurance Company was not without many losses, being at a set disadvantage by providing life insurance for African Americans that were denied by other places because of their skin colour [7].

By leading this company from the beginning to the middle of the 20th century, Spaulding showed that the children of Emancipation could live wealthy lives despite the very core of their society working against them.

In ‘Re-discovering Charles Clinton Spaulding’s “The Administration of Big Business”’, it is stated that, “Spaulding’s contributions were significant, from his insight regarding the fundamental necessities for the effective management of a business to his management style and implementation of practices…” [3], showing that Spaulding accrued a great deal of wisdom throughout his career and educated the masses of his community; through generous philanthropy to great HBCUs and his position as director of many other financial institutions. The paper then goes on to agree with the title of ‘Father of African-American Management’ [3] for Spaulding, given with absolute confidence due to his dedication to the support of those who strive for the level at which Spaulding propelled him and his company to.
