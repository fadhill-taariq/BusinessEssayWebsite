Charles Clinton Spaulding - The Father of Black Business

Welcome to my blog! - Taariq Fadhill

Conclusion

To conclude, Charles Clinton Spaulding was a man who stopped at nothing to reach his level of success and once at the top, encouraged and nurtured the next generation of black business to flourish with his teachings. As an adept business thinker, Spaulding’s words will continue to be a point of reference for generations to come; for following his rules and ideals means to one day, be as successful as ‘Mr. Negro Business’ [18] himself.

Through this analysis of Spaulding’s work and achievements, I can safely say that his dedication to the elevation of the African American business environment was successful and immensely long-lasting. While his legacy may not be as eternal as his ‘father of African American management’ [3] title may suggest, Spaulding’s teachings propelled Durham, North Carolina, to house the ‘black Wall Street’ [1] and was a feat that has rooted Spaulding as a business mastermind. His thought pieces will remain a vital part of the development of future black entrepreneurs both as a resource and an ideology to live by for decades to come.
